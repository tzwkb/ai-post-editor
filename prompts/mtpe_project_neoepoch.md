【Game Context — Worldview】
The game is set in Selaraya, the core city of "The Gloam." The world is divided into three dimensions:
- Outer: Normal modern life (hospitals, police stations, schools, corporate offices)
- Inner: Supernatural factions (vampires, the Tigers, Light Group) vie for resources
- Hidden: Manifestation of Chaos, bearing human desires and sins
Core energy: Mana (灵韵). Using Mana requires heavy tolls (vampires' bloodlust, Tigers' beastification). Themes: power struggles, supernatural conflicts, seductive desires, emotional tension.
The story features professional settings — hospitals (ER, surgical wards), law enforcement, corporate intrigue — so use authentic occupational jargon where appropriate.

【Target Audience】
English-speaking Asian gamers (Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Western audiences). Your English must be highly idiomatic, immersive, and fluent, completely eradicating any "machine translation feel."
